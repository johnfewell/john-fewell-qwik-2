# Lucid Drift

*An algorithmic philosophy for the Lucid 2026 portfolio background*

## The Movement

Lucid Drift is the computational aesthetics of the hypnagogic state — the threshold
where waking attention dissolves into dream imagery. It holds that a background must
behave like a dream being watched: vast, slow, self-organizing, and indifferent to
the viewer, yet alive enough that the eye catches it moving when it looks away from
the text. Nothing in Lucid Drift is keyframed. Every motion is the consequence of a
field, and every field is the consequence of a seed. The work never repeats and never
ends; it is a state, not a clip.

## Process Over Product

The substance of Lucid Drift is a continuous vector field built from layered gradient
noise, slowly rotating through time as though the dream itself were breathing.
Hundreds of weightless particles are born into this field, surrender to it completely,
and leave behind luminous trails — wisps — that accumulate, veil, and dissolve. The
trails are the artwork; the particles are merely the pen. Constructive overlap of
wisps produces nodes of brightness the way overlapping thoughts produce moments of
clarity, and the slow erosion of old trails enacts forgetting. The composition at any
instant is unplanned and unplannable: it emerges from force balance alone, the way a
master generative artist insists it must, with every constant tuned through countless
iterations until chaos reads as intention.

## The Field Breathes

Time enters the system as a third noise dimension, so the field does not scroll —
it *metamorphoses*. Calm laminar regions shear into turbulent eddies and relax again
over periods measured in minutes, echoing the two-minute sine wander already living
in the site's haze mask. Velocity maps to luminance: fast wisps burn toward the warm
end of the palette, slow wisps cool and fade. The palette is never invented by the
algorithm; it is inherited from the surface it haunts — sunset coral, magenta, cyan,
and teal by day; deep indigo, violet, and phosphorescent cyan by night — so the
generative layer and the designed layer read as one continuous material.

## Seeded Variation

Every run is reproducible from a single integer. The seed deterministically fixes the
noise lattice, the birth positions, the per-particle phase offsets, and the palette
weighting, so seed 47 is always the same dream and seed 48 is always a different one.
This is the Art Blocks discipline: infinite variation, total accountability. The
parameter space — particle population, field scale, drift speed, trail persistence,
turbulence depth — is the instrument; the seed is the score. Exploration happens by
walking the seed line, not by editing code.

## The Conceptual Seed

The quiet reference is lucid dreaming itself. In a lucid dream the dreamer's attention
is a spotlight: imagery sharpens where awareness lands and blurs into haze everywhere
else. The site already has that spotlight — the wandering radial mask that thins the
overlay. Lucid Drift lives *beneath* the haze, so its wisps are vivid inside the
attention spot and submerged everywhere else. Anyone who has practiced lucidity will
feel the metaphor; everyone else simply sees a beautiful, breathing background.

## Craftsmanship

The final implementation must feel like the product of deep computational expertise —
painstaking optimization, master-level restraint. Concretely: trails fade by alpha
erosion rather than repainting, so the layer composites over the living gradient
without ever occluding it; the device pixel ratio is capped; the simulation sleeps
when the tab is hidden; and the entire system stands down for viewers who prefer
reduced motion. A meticulously crafted algorithm is one whose constants no longer
look like choices — they look like physics.
